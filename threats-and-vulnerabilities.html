Malware: Malicious software like viruses, worms, Trojans, ransomware, and spyware.

Phishing: Deceptive attempts to obtain sensitive information by pretending to be a trustworthy entity.

DDoS Attacks: Distributed Denial of Service attacks overwhelm a network with traffic, causing disruption.

Zero-day Exploits: Vulnerabilities that are unknown to those interested in mitigating the vulnerability.
